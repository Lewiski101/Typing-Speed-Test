How to Clean Your Keyboard

Published on November 3, 2025

A clean keyboard is a happy keyboard. Here's how to keep yours free of dust, crumbs, and other debris.

1. Unplug Your Keyboard

Before you start cleaning, always unplug your keyboard from your computer.

2. Shake It Out

Turn your keyboard upside down and gently shake it to remove any loose debris.

3. Use Compressed Air

Use a can of compressed air to blow out any remaining dust and crumbs from between the keys.

4. Wipe It Down

Wipe the keys and the surface of the keyboard with a soft, lint-free cloth lightly dampened with water or isopropyl alcohol.
