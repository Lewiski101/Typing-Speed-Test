Stretches for Your Hands and Wrists

Published on November 3, 2025

Typing for long periods can cause strain in your hands and wrists. These simple stretches can help relieve tension.

1. Wrist Flexion and Extension

Extend your arm in front of you with your palm facing up. Gently bend your wrist down with your other hand. Hold for 15-30 seconds. Repeat with your palm facing down.

2. Finger Stretches

Gently spread your fingers apart and hold for a few seconds. Then, make a fist and hold. Repeat several times.

3. Thumb Stretches

Gently pull your thumb back towards your wrist. Hold for 15-30 seconds.
