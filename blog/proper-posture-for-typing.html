Proper Posture for Typing

Published on November 3, 2025

Good posture is essential for comfortable and efficient typing. Here's how to sit correctly at your desk.

1. Sit Up Straight

Your back should be straight and your shoulders should be relaxed.

2. Keep Your Feet Flat on the Floor

If your feet don't reach the floor, use a footrest.

3. Position Your Elbows at a 90-Degree Angle

Your forearms should be parallel to the floor.

4. Keep Your Wrists Straight

Avoid bending your wrists up, down, or to the side.
