Understanding Keyboard Switches

Published on November 3, 2025

The switches are the components that register a keystroke. There are three main types of switches.

Linear Switches

Linear switches are smooth and consistent. They are a popular choice for gamers.

Tactile Switches

Tactile switches provide a noticeable bump when you press them. They are a good choice for typists.

Clicky Switches

Clicky switches provide both a tactile bump and an audible click. They are a popular choice for people who enjoy the sound and feel of a typewriter.
