5 Exercises to Improve Typing Speed

Published on November 3, 2025

Improving your typing speed requires consistent practice. Here are five exercises you can do to boost your WPM and accuracy.

1. Home Row Drills

Practice typing combinations of the home row keys (asdf jkl;) to build muscle memory.

2. Alphabet Drills

Type the alphabet from A to Z. This helps you practice reaching for all the keys.

3. Common Word Drills

Practice typing the most common words in English, such as "the," "be," "to," "of," and "and."

4. Pangram Practice

Pangrams are sentences that contain every letter of the alphabet. "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog" is a classic example.

5. Timed Tests

Use a typing test like TypeMaster to measure your progress and challenge yourself to improve your score.
