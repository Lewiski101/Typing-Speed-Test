Keyboard Layouts Explained

Published on November 3, 2025

Most people are familiar with the QWERTY keyboard layout, but did you know there are alternatives? This post will explore some of the most common keyboard layouts.

QWERTY

The QWERTY layout is the most common keyboard layout in the world. It was designed in the 1870s to prevent typewriter jams.

Dvorak

The Dvorak layout was designed to be more ergonomic and efficient than QWERTY. It places the most commonly used letters on the home row.

Colemak

The Colemak layout is a modern alternative to both QWERTY and Dvorak. It is designed to be easy to learn for existing QWERTY users.
