Setting Up Your Workspace for Optimal Typing

Published on November 3, 2025

Your workspace setup can have a big impact on your typing comfort and health. Here are some tips for creating an ergonomic workspace.

1. Adjust Your Chair Height

Your feet should be flat on the floor and your knees should be at a 90-degree angle.

2. Position Your Monitor

The top of your monitor should be at or slightly below eye level.

3. Use a Keyboard Tray

A keyboard tray can help you position your keyboard at the correct height and angle.

4. Keep Everything Within Reach

Your mouse, phone, and other frequently used items should be within easy reach to avoid stretching and straining.
