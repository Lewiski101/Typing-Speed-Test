How to Type Faster: Proven Tips and Techniques

Published on November 3, 2025

Typing faster is a goal for many computer users. Here are some proven tips to help you increase your WPM.

1. Learn Touch Typing

As mentioned in our beginner's guide, touch typing is the most important skill for fast typing.

2. Use a Typing Tutor

Online typing tutors provide structured lessons and exercises to help you learn and practice.

3. Practice Regularly

Consistency is key. Set aside time each day to practice your typing skills.

4. Focus on Accuracy

Don't sacrifice accuracy for speed. The fewer mistakes you make, the less time you'll spend correcting them.

5. Get a Good Keyboard

A comfortable, high-quality keyboard can make a significant difference in your typing speed and comfort.
