Common Typing Mistakes and How to Avoid Them

Published on November 3, 2025

Even experienced typists make mistakes. Here are some of the most common errors and how to correct them.

1. Bottom Row Errors

The bottom row of the keyboard (Z, X, C, V, B, N, M) is often the most difficult to master. Practice drills that focus on these keys.

2. Capitalization Errors

Forgetting to use the Shift key for capital letters is a common mistake. Practice typing sentences that include proper nouns and the beginning of sentences.

3. Punctuation Errors

Incorrectly placed commas, periods, and other punctuation marks can change the meaning of a sentence. Pay close attention to punctuation as you type.

4. Homophone Errors

Words that sound the same but are spelled differently (e.g., "their," "there," "they're") are a common source of confusion. Proofread your work carefully to catch these mistakes.
