The Importance of Ergonomics in Typing

Published on November 3, 2025

Ergonomics is the science of designing and arranging things so that people can use them easily and safely. When it comes to typing, proper ergonomics can prevent injuries and improve your performance.

1. Maintain Good Posture

Sit up straight with your back against your chair. Your feet should be flat on the floor.

2. Position Your Keyboard Correctly

Your keyboard should be at a height that allows your elbows to be bent at a 90-degree angle.

3. Keep Your Wrists Straight

Avoid bending your wrists up or down. A wrist rest can help you maintain a neutral position.

4. Take Regular Breaks

Get up and stretch every 30 minutes to an hour to prevent muscle strain.
