The Importance of Typing Speed

In today's digital world, typing is a fundamental skill. A faster typing speed can save you hours of time, whether you're a student, a professional, or just communicating online. The average typing speed is around 40 WPM, but professionals often type at 65-75 WPM. Improving your speed can significantly boost your productivity.

Faster typing isn't just about speed; it's about efficiency. It allows your thoughts to flow directly to the screen, making writing and coding more seamless.
